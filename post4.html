Live Life!

Life is a wild ride. It will whip you around from side to side.
Take you and drop you from way up high. But also give you the feeling as though you can fly, The adrenaline rush is like no other
Making you speechless at times or maybe even stutter. There is no greater ride to take. The decision is so easy to make. So then why do so many of us skip this ride?. Don’t you want to be able to say, “At least I tried”?.
Too many people are scared of the drops. Or scared of being so high up that their ear pops. Some people are impatient and don’t want to wait in line. They don’t realize that good things take time. Some people are scared of how fast it can go. Some even say it’s over before you know. What’s unique about this ride is that it changes every day
